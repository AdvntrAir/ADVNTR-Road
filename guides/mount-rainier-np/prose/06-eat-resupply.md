### RESUPPLY NOTES

Let's be direct: nobody comes to Mount Rainier for a fine dining awakening. Provisioning here is a strict tactical exercise.

If you are tackling the northeast corridor along WA-410, Enumclaw is your final stand for true civilization. Hit the Safeway, top off your propane tanks, and fill your diesel tanks at standard valley prices before you start climbing into the mountains. On the southwest side along WA-706, handle your major grocery runs in Eatonville or Puyallup. Once you pass the mountain foothills, grocery selection plummets and prices skyrocket. Ashford features basic general stores, but they charge a steep premium for their proximity to the park gates.

The most critical logistical reality for 2026 is waste management. With all interior national park dump stations dark this season, you have to treat your gateway towns as utility hubs. Packwood RV Park and the municipal stations in Enumclaw are no longer just conveniences-they are infrastructure lifelines that you must plan around to keep your rig operational.

Cell service is inconsistent, especially west of Lake Crescent and along the coast. Download maps, reservation details, tide tables, and permit confirmations before leaving town.

Weather can change quickly across short distances. A sunny morning in Port Angeles does not promise a dry afternoon in the Hoh. Pack layers, keep rain gear accessible, and treat seasonal road notices as current until proven otherwise.

The practical work is not glamorous: fees, passes, tide tables, pet rules, road alerts, and backup food. Olympic rewards the traveler who handles those things early and then leaves space for the day to change.

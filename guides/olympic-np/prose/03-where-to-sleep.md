Campgrounds in Olympic are defined by access as much as scenery. A site in the right drainage can save hours of backtracking. A beautiful site in the wrong corner can turn every day into a commute.

Use the cards below as the structured starting set. Hoh and Kalaloch are the most legible choices for many first-time trips: one puts you inside the rain forest, the other gives the coast a practical base. Sol Duc, Mora, and Heart O' the Hills matter too, but their usefulness depends heavily on the route you are building.

The main rule is simple: sleep near tomorrow morning, not near yesterday afternoon.

Hurricane Ridge is the cleanest way to understand Olympic's vertical range. The road climbs quickly out of Port Angeles, and suddenly the park is no longer moss and tidewater but open sky, wind, and ridgelines.

This is a clear-morning zone. If the forecast gives you a window, take it early. Afternoon cloud, traffic, and weather can turn a confident plan into a slow retreat.

Deer Park is the rougher alpine counterpoint. It is narrower, less forgiving, and better matched to smaller vehicles and drivers who are comfortable with exposure. For many RV travelers, Hurricane Ridge is the better answer.

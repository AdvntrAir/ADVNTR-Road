Lake Crescent is the pause between the northern gateway and the wetter west side. It is easy to treat it as a drive-by lake, but that undersells what it does for a trip: it gives the day a quieter center.

Sol Duc is more of a commitment. The valley pulls you off the main road toward forest, campground logistics, and the classic waterfall walk. The hot springs add convenience for some travelers and crowds for others.

This zone works best when you stop trying to make it only a transfer day. Give the lake or the valley enough room to be the thing you came for.

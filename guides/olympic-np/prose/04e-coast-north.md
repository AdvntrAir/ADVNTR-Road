The north coast asks for more time. Mora and Rialto are the easiest pieces to fold into a road trip, especially from Forks. Ozette and Shi Shi push farther into the edge of the map and reward travelers who are not trying to do everything in one day.

Tides matter more here. So do permits, road time, and realistic expectations about wet gear. A beautiful beach can still be a bad idea at the wrong tide.

Treat this zone as its own day when possible. It is the wrong place to rush.

Resupply is easiest before you commit to the west side. Port Angeles has the deepest bench for groceries, gear, fuel, and sit-down meals. Forks covers the essentials and is often the practical reset between rain forest and coast days.

Summer dinner reservations are worth making for lodge dining. For camp cooking, assume the next good grocery stop may be farther away than it looks.

Do not save every errand for the last town before the campground. On the peninsula, one closed counter or one late arrival can turn a simple grocery stop into dinner from the bottom of the snack bag.

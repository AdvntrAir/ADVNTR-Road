Olympic is not one park so much as three strong personalities sharing the same border. The high country moves on mountain weather. The west side belongs to rain forest, river gravel, and long green shade. The coast runs by tide tables and patience.

Start by choosing your anchor, not your checklist. A Port Angeles base makes Hurricane Ridge and Lake Crescent easier. Forks puts you closer to the Hoh, Sol Duc, Mora, and Rialto. Kalaloch works when the coast is the point.

> The mistake is trying to make Olympic small enough for one perfect loop.
